Check-in #2

Results focused


Theory

Congratulations! You're really into the thick of this process applying for jobs and receiving invitations for interviews. The big idea to keep in mind at this point is to be results focused. What does that mean?

Whichever way the wind is blowing during this process, you want to have a good pulse on that. That's accomplished with documentation of your applications and the events that unfold therefrom.

Naturally, you'll apply to dozens of jobs and you'll only get a couple offers. The probabilities at each stage of this process for each job don't favor the individual. It's with volume (enough applications) that those probabilities, in the aggregate, sum to enough bites at the apple.

In summary: keep a pulse on the results of your search, ready to change your strategy given the trends.


Practice

As mentioned in a previous section, document this funnel, these probabilities in a spreadsheet like the below. You'll be able to tell, over time, if you're getting enough phone interviews, if enough of those are turning into interpersonal interviews, etc.

You've worked too hard on your content, too hard applying for jobs to not know exactly what's happening. Take the time to document!
