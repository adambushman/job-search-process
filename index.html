About

On Your Terms was born from the idea that everyone should be able to seize the reins of their professional future, no matter how traditional or unconvention your path to date.

This resource is FREE, intended to help anyone perform a job search correctly and avoid pitfalls that could deter finding that dream job.

On Your Terms was written by Adam Bushman, a data enthusiast passionate about inspiring others to properly invest in their future and define their career as they see fit.
