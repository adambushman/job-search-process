Applying

Don'ts


Theory

The big thing to avoid in applications is self-DE-selection.

For many companies, their job posting is a "wish list", not a "must list". Furthermore, listings aren't perfectly indicative of the job itself.

Also, don't be satisfied with just "X" applications. Keep that pipeline filled. You want choices in your job search and that requires a wide opening at the start of the funnel.

No summary


Practice

It's not complicated, just apply and let the process do its thing.


Application

  1. Don't fall for the trap of "I don't qualify" or "I probably can't do that job"; let the process determine that
