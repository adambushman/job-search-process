Interviews

Phone


Theory

Hiring managers or HR reps just waded through up to hundreds of resumes, cover letters, etc. They likely just eliminated 80% of them. The fact you got a phone interview is a big deal.

Still, that leaves a lot of phone interviews left. These will last 15-30 mins and are pretty rapid fire. The ultimate goal is to fill in the gaps left over from the assets relative to the company's wish list.

Similarly, you likely have observed gabs between your "must haves"/"nice to haves" and what the listing described. This is your chance to finish evaluating the opportunity!


Practice

Let's dig into each interview type specifically and what you should do to prepare:


Phone interview

Let's run through some principles of the phone interview:

  • Come prepared enough about the company and the listing to not mix up Cricut (the crafting tool/software with the cell phone network provider...yeah, that happened to me)
  • Be concise and to the point. This ensures both sides fill in the gaps in the required time.
  • Come with your own questions! Hopefully you prepped these but they can take two forms:
    1. Aligning company need with personal value
    2. Painting a correct picture of the opportunity relative to your "must haves"/"nice to haves"
