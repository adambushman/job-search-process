Why

Ready to commit


Theory

Have you ever noticed that when you're driving in the car and begin a turn your body naturally leans the direction it was traveling before the change. That's called inertia. The path of least resistance is to keep on the direction and speed our body had become accustomed to. It takes energy to counter inertia in physics. We can view this process through a similar lens.

In summary: consider that what's possible in your job search may primarily be a function of time.


Practice

Similarly, keeping on with the inertia of present life is the easiest path forward and any change will require great effort. This process isn't as simple as just dusting off a five-year-old resume and casually applying to a couple jobs on your phone while soaking in the bath. It'll take real commitment to effect this change.

Ready to commit?


Application
