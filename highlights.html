Highlights

Theory


If you're a sports fan, you likely enjoy team, game, and player highlights of the action, even if you watched the original contest live. By definition, highlights are the most memorable moments from an event.

Looking forward to your inevitable interviews, employers more than likely won't remember a lot. You want to give them something unforgettable. Highlights from you experience, presented in an engaging, value-driven narrative will achieve just that.

In summary: people seek out highlights, so give employers just that.
